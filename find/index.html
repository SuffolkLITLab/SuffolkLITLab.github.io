How to find The Lab

The Lab (685E) is hidden away on the 6th floor of Sargent Hall inside the Suffolk Law library at 120 Tremont Street, Boston, MA 02108. Enter the library (left as you exit the elevator). Take a hard right, then a left at the microfiche. Pass through room 675. We're in suite 685.
