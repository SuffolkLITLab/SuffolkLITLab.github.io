LIT Bits

LIT Bits are rapid-fire talks focusing on legal innovation and technology where the speaker is given only 5 min and 20 slides to get their point across. The slides are set to advance automatically every 15 seconds. What follows is a collection of LIT Bits from events hosted by the Lab, mostly our annual Clinnovation conference.

ProTip: You can make a video fill your screen by clicking on the full screen button at the bottom right of the video. This should appear once you start playing the video.
