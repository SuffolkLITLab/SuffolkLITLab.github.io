Using Inkscape to Create and Edit Vector Graphics

Estimated Time (Reading & Exercises): ~30 min.

You are welcome to join our Slack Team. There you can ask and answer questions relating to this lesson under the #howto channel. See How To for more. VIDEO PROTIP: After starting playback, you can expand a video to full-screen by mousing over it and clicking the full-screen icon. You can also speed up playback by clicking the gear icon, choosing "playback speed," and picking your speed.

First, download and install Inkscape. Watch the first video below to get a feel for Inkscape and learn some of the nomenclature. Then try your hand at producing an image from scratch by following along with the second video.
